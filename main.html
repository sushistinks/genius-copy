"Meat Grinder", like any other track MF DOOM is part of, shows off his extremely rhyme heavy bars, as well as his references to obscure pop culture.
The track’s subjects range from DOOM hopelessly trying to score with a young girl, to him deprecating himself for his shoe style, and overall, just bragging about his insane lyrics.
